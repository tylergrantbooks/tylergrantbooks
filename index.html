About Tyler

Tyler Grant is the author of dozens of comedy, suspense, and horror stories, including the popular “Tyler Grant’s Series of Voices”. He holds a Master’s in English and worked as a news journalist for six years prior to writing short fiction and novels. You can follow him on Facebook at fb.com/Tyler-Grant-Books. Be sure to like his page, and follow for updates regarding his latest work. Grant lives in Washington State.
